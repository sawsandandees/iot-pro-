Our web application is designed to monitor gas levels and temperature in your kitchen, ensuring a safe and secure environment. It utilizes smart sensors to detect gas leaks and temperature variations, providing real-time insights to protect your home.

The application is linked to sensors installed in your kitchen. These sensors continuously monitor gas levels and temperature. In the event of a gas leak or high temperature, the application sends instant notifications to your preferred device, keeping you informed and allowing for prompt action.

- Real-time gas level monitoring. - Temperature tracking to prevent overheating. - Instant notifications via web and mobile app. - User-friendly interface for easy interaction. - Historical data analysis for trends and insights.

Accessing the application is simple. You can log in through any web browser or use our dedicated mobile app. Both platforms provide seamless access to real-time monitoring, historical data, and notifications.
